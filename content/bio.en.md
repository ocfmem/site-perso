## Once upon a time

In 1998, I had the Internet for a few weeks, I created my very first web page in pure HTML, with a photo and a little bit of badly formatted text, all without any sense of design. I put it online. I remember the excitement I felt when I told myself that from my room, as a high school student, I had created something that anyone, even on the other side of the world, could access.

I think it was on that day that a virus infected me — a digital virus, of course. It has developed over the years and pushed me towards software development, the creation of applications and the implementation of new digital services: everything that has driven me for 20 years. And even today, with each new release, I feel a little of this same excitement at the idea of ​​showing a project that I have worked on. And each time, I have the hope that it will improve the daily life of someone, wherever they are in the world.

I'm **Meddy Menzikoff**.
I develop software for people around the world.
